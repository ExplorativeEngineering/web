Light Field Projection of 3D Emissive Objects
This models a fluorescence light field mircoscope where each of the points (voxels) within the 3D objects emit light which projected through a microlens array to the camera sensor.

This is part of Rudolf Oldenbourg's project: Polarized Light Field Microscope using Deep Learning
